About Us

Paw Darshan Nepal is dedicated to improving the lives of street dogs across Nepal. We connect these deserving animals with loving foster homes and forever families, providing them with the care, love, and stability they deserve.

Our network of volunteers, foster families, and supporters work together to make a meaningful difference in the lives of these animals. Join us in our mission to create a more compassionate world for street dogs!

How It Works

Report

Spot a street dog in need? Take a picture and share the location with us so our team can help.

Foster

Volunteer to provide a temporary home or find foster homes for dogs while they await adoption.

Adopt

Give a rescued dog a permanent, loving home and gain a loyal companion for life.
