How sisters are important
Support: Sisters can provide advice, support, and laughter.
Encouragement: Sisters can encourage you to learn and grow.
Inspiration: Sisters can inspire you with their achievements and contributions to the world.
Grounding: Sisters can keep you grounded, no matter where life takes you.
Role models: Older sisters can act as role models and teachers who help younger siblings learn about many things.
Mental health: Having a sister can protect adolescents against feeling lonely, unloved, guilty, self-conscious, and fearful.
Development: Older sisters engage in more stimulating activities with their younger siblings than older brothers do.

Sisters are important in life because they provide emotional support, companionship, and a sense of security.

Sisters often share a deep emotional connection and understanding of one another that can transcend all other relationships.
